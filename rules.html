 Game Rules: 

  1. Enter your name to start the game.
  2. Set a maximum range for the random number.
  3. The game will generate a random number between 1 and your maximum range.
  4. Try to guess the number by entering your guess.
  5. If your guess is correct, you will receive a congratulatory message and your score will be displayed.
  6. If your guess is too low or too high, you will receive hints to try again.
  7. You can refresh the game at any time to start over.
